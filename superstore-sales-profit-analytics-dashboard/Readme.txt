# Superstore Sales & Profit Analytics Dashboard

An interactive Tableau Business Intelligence project designed to analyze sales, profit, customer behavior, product performance, and regional trends using the Superstore dataset.

## Dashboards Included

### Executive Dashboard

* KPI tracking
* Monthly sales & profit trends
* Regional performance analysis
* Category-wise sales insights

### Product Performance Dashboard

* Top-selling products
* Most profitable products
* Loss-making products
* Category analysis

### Customer Analytics Dashboard

* Top customers by sales
* Segment-wise analysis
* Customer trends
* Repeat customer insights

### Regional Analytics Dashboard

* Regional sales & profit analysis
* Top states performance
* Geographic insights
* Profitability analysis
